Stretch Reminder

Instructions for Usage

This menu is on the upper right corner of your browser.

"Get an exercise now" opens a new notification page. The notification page provides you with a stretch, instructions, a link for more exercises, and a link to talk to a physical therapist for more information.

"Upper Body," "Every 30 minutes," and "Enabled" represent options. There are three options: area of exercise, frequency, and enabled/disabled. Clicking on an options button loops through the options. The extension will run what is currently displayed. The options are as follows.

Area of Exercise: Upper Body, Lower Body, or Full Body.

Frequency: Every 30, 60, or 120 minutes.

Enabled or Disabled.
